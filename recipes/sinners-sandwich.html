Sinners Sandwich

Anyone who's played Deadly Premonition should remember this strange yet oddly tasty sandwich that detective York Morgan once tried.

Below you will find the how to guide for recreating it.

Ingredients

  • A loaf of White Crustless Bread
  • 2 Turkey Breasts
  • A Jar of High Quality Strawberry Jam or Conserve
  • Cereal, preferably grain based
  • Butter or Margerine
    •

    Instructions

    1. Cook breasts

      2. I bought my turkey breast fresh and cooked it slowly in the oven with a little butter - but feel free to use pre-cooked chunks, not processed slices.

    2. Assembled bread

      3. Get two pieces of bread; spread a little butter on one and the strawberry conserve liberally on the other.

    3. Cut Breasts

      4. Cut the turkey breast into medium slices at an angle and lay the pieces onto the buttered piece of bread.

    4. Sprinkle Cereal

      5. Pick a cereal of your choice and sprinkle generously over the conserve.

    5. Assembled sandwich

      6. Place the second piece of bread strawberry side down on top and cut the sandwich diagonally to serve.
