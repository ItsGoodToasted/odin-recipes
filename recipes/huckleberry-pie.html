Huckleberry Pie

A fantastic recipe for a huckleberry-pie

Just follow the steps below to enjoy this wonderful dish

Ingredients

  • 2 9" pie crusts
  • 4 cups huckleberries
  • 1 tsp grated lemon zest
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1 tsp white sugar

Instructions:

  1. Prep the pie tin

    2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Line a pie tin with a pie crust. Add huckleberries into pie crust.

  2. Mix the filling

    3. In a small bowl, mix together 3/4 cups of sugar and flour. Spoon evenly over berries. Sprinkle lemon juice and zest over the top. Then dot with butter.

  3. Add the top crust

    4. Add top crust, making sure to seal the edges. Gently seal the edges by pressing the crust down with your fingers. Gaps in the crust will result in huckleberry juice creeping out.

  4. Cut seam vents

    5. Cut seam vents in the top crust. Brush the top of the pie with heavy cream and sprinkle on 1 teaspoon of sugar. The heavy cream will give the pie a nice golden brown finish.

  5. Bake the pie

    6. Cut seam vents in the top crust. Brush the top of the pie with heavy cream and sprinkle on 1 teaspoon of sugar. The heavy cream will give the pie a nice golden brown finish.
