Rum and Coke sans Coke

My go to recipe for when you need a cocktail but didn't have the energy to run to Walgreens.

The recipe for this couldn't be easier. Just follow the steps below!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite rum, preferably spiced.
  • A glass (optional)
  • Ice (optional)
    •

    Instructions

    1. Add ice to your glass of choice. If you don't have ice, skip to step 2.
    2. Pour you rum into the glass. No glass? Skip straight to skip 3.
    3. Enjoy your rum, sans coke
    4. Get ready for the longest night of your life, and the worst day ahead.
